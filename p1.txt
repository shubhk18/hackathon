Notes:
	•	The  ExceptionListener  in the JMS client captures disconnects.
	•	 ListenerContainer  manages and restarts threads as needed for concurrency.
	•	Automatic reconnect is available via properties on the client connection factory.
	•	This pattern matches enterprise JMS listener implementations for IBM MQ
